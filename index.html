Creating the voice I wish I had.

Who I am

Born in Rome to Sri Lankan parents.

Moved to London when I was thirteen.

It was difficult. Strange.

I had to become the leader in my family overnight.

Documents. Visas. Everything.

I watched how much my parents sacrificed.

I started trying things.

Got scammed. Got depressed. Nearly gave up on everything.

I started drinking just to fit in.

The wake up

When I was 17, I visited where my parents grew up.

I call it my wake up.

I came back and said: say yes to everything.

See what happens.

Three years later
I'm still
saying yes.

Now I document my journey for the people who were where I was.

Lost. No clue what they want. Afraid to try.

My mission is simple.

Create the voice I wish I had.
