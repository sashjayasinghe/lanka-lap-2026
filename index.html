Born in Rome to Sri Lankan parents.

Moved to London at 13.

Spent years learning to fit in.

That skill didn't transfer.

Where I came from

In Italy I was the brown kid.

In London I was a coconut —

Brown on the outside, white on the inside.

Too western for South Asian spaces.

Too foreign for everyone else.

My family

They came from Sri Lanka.

They built a life somewhere else.

I grew up in between.

I spent years looking for a room

where that wasn't the problem.

I never found it.
