Why I'm
Doing This.

Born in Rome to Sri Lankan parents. Moved to London when I was thirteen. It was difficult. Strange. I had to become the leader in my family overnight. Documents. Visas. Everything. I watched how much my parents sacrificed. I started trying things.
Got scammed. Got depressed.
Nearly gave up on everything. I started drinking just to fit in. Then at 17 I visited where my parents grew up. I call it my wake up. I came back and said: say yes to everything. See what happens. Three years later I'm still saying yes. Now I document my journey for the people who were where I was. Lost. No clue what they want. Afraid to try. My mission: create the voice I wish I had. The run is how I get the world to pay attention.
What this starts

The Vision.

The run is the beginning. Not the goal.

01
The Run Clubs

Five clubs launched while running — one for each community across the island. With Nūl, they become permanent infrastructure. Not a one-off event.

02
Retreats & Masterminds

For the diaspora — people like me, who grew up abroad and lost the thread back to their culture. Hosted on Nūl's land in Sri Lanka. The room I never found.

03
4Lanka Marathon

An annual marathon — Sri Lanka and the diaspora on the same finish line. One step at a time. This is the long game.
