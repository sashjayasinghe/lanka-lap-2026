Why I'm
doing this.

Born in Rome to Sri Lankan parents. Moved to London when I was thirteen. It was difficult. Strange. I had to become the leader in my family overnight. Documents. Visas. Everything. I watched how much my parents sacrificed. I started trying things. Got scammed.
Got depressed.
Nearly gave up on everything. I started drinking just to fit in. When I was 17, I visited where my parents grew up. I call it my wake up. I came back and said: say yes to everything. See what happens. Three years later I'm still saying yes. I document my journey for the people who were where I was. Lost. No clue what they want. Afraid to try. My mission: create the voice I wish I had. The run is how I get the world to pay attention.
